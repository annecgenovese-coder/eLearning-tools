Step 1
  1. Understand the Problem (Frame/Analyze)
    • Identify the Core Issue: Go beyond symptoms to find the underlying root cause.
    • Gather Data: Analyze facts, numbers, and data to remove emotion and objective bias.
    • Define Constraints: Identify what is known, unknown, and any restrictions (budget, time)
      .
Step 2
Develop a Strategy (Explore/Plan)
  • Brainstorm Alternatives: Generate multiple potential solutions.
  • Select the Best Approach: Evaluate solutions against constraints identified in Step 1.
  • Map the Process: Determine the necessary steps to implement the solution
Step 3
Implement the Solution (Execute)
  • Take Action: Execute the chosen strategy.
  • Monitor Results: Check that the solution effectively addresses the problem and make adjustments if necessary.
  • Secure Buy-in: Ensure stakeholders are aligned and onboard with the solution
